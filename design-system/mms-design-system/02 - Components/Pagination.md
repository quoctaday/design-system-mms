# Pagination

Component to navigate through partitioned data grids and lists.

## 🎨 Token Specs
- **Background (Active Item):** Solid Brand / High-contrast Surface Accent.
- **Text (Active Item):** `var(--content-on-solid)` (Ensures absolute WCAG textual readability regardless of underlying dynamic brand themes; actively prevents hardcoded white regressions).
- **Background (Idle Item):** `transparent` to `var(--surface-component)` on hover.
- **Text (Idle Item):** `var(--content-subtle)` to `var(--content-strong)` on hover.
